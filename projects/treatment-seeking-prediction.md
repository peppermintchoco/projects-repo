# Mental Health Treatment Seeking Behavior Prediction

## Overview
This projects explores factors influencing mental health treatment-seeking behavior among tech employees using a simple logistic regression model.

## Objectives
- Clean and prepare the dataset to ensure accuracy and usability.
- Perform EDA to explore relationships between factors and mental health help-seeking behavior.
- Build and test a logistic regression model to investigate significant predictors in help-seeking behavior.

## Methods & Tools
- Python, Pandas, Numpy
- Data cleaning and encoding
- Visualization: Matplotlib, Seaborn
- Statistical summaries and correlation heatmaps
- Scikit-learn (Logistic Regression)
- Jupyter Notebook for analysis and interpretation

## Key Findings
- The model achieved an accuracy of 0.69 and an AUC of 0.73.
